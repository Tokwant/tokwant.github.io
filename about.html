About Our Tofu Squares

Tokwant is a local snack brand that turns simple tofu into something fun, flavorful, and unforgettable. Our crispy-on-the-outside, soft-on-the-inside tofu squares are made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients and paired with exciting dips like sisig sauce, cheese sauce, and garlic mayo.

Perfect for students, professionals, and families alike, Tokwant makes healthy eating both accessible and enjoyable. With affordable prices, and customizable flavors, we're here to bring protein-packed snacking to canteens, campuses, and online platforms—one tasty square at a time.
